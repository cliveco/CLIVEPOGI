Never leave flames unattended		 Make sure to keep an eye on anything that involves a flame or a lot of heat, whether you're cooking, lighting candles, or doing anything else similar. Keep flammable items away from heat
Always keep flammable materials away from sources of high heat or flame, such as fabrics, paper, and even hair. When lighting a fire in your fireplace, you should avoid having your hair or clothes in the way.

Keep oil and gasses away from flames.		 The gas should not be near a flame or spark that might ignite.
